Frequently asked questions

What other types of flexible financing does Nordea offer?
We also offer hire purchase agreements. 
With a hire purchase agreement, we buy item that you wish to acquire, and you repay us in monthly installments. When the full amount is paid off, you own the item you bought through this agreement. 
To find out more, either log in to Nordea Business where you can read more and apply, or contact our advisors to discuss how this might work for your company.

How do I apply for financing?
To apply for financing, you must be a Nordea Customer. If you are not already a corporate customer, you can apply to become one online. 
If you are already a customer, you can read about and apply for a selection of our financing options under the 'Service' tab in Nordea Business. 
Join us to get financing for your company
Apply for financing in Nordea Business
What do we need you to send to us along with your application?
When you apply for financing, you will need to send the following along with your application: 
 * Your business plan and budget calculation, if you are starting a new business.
 * Information about your company’s ownership structure.
 * Your company’s description and budget for next year.
 * Financial statements (total profit and balance sheet) or the operating income from last year.

When can I expect to hear from you?
We will contact you as soon as we've handled your application.

What financial risk management does Nordea offer?
As a corporate customer at Nordea, you can get expert help to streamline your company's risk management. Our experts are ready to advise you on currency hedging, debt management and much more.
