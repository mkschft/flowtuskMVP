About Us
========

![Image 15](https://www.novotel-bd.com/images/ico_aboutus.png)Expanding telecommunication 

infrastructures and 

establishing relationships

NovoTel Limited is the licensed International Gateway (IGW) operator of Bangladesh Telecommunication Regulatory Commission (BTRC) to terminate international voice traffic to and from Bangladesh.

NovoTel started its journey with the mission to expand telecommunication infrastructures that ensure quality services to establish long-term rewarding relationship with its partners by adding value.
