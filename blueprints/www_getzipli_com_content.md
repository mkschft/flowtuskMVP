# Zipli Overview

## Core Offering
Zipli is a B2B platform designed to reduce food waste and improve sustainability within the food industry and municipalities. It provides a mobile app and an AI-powered data platform that facilitates the efficient redistribution of surplus food from businesses to organizations in need.

## Key Features and Benefits
- **Real-Time Food Tracking**: Allows users to post donations or food requests, matching surplus food with those in need instantly.
- **Automated Redistribution Logistics**: Simplifies the logistics of food donations, ensuring compliance with food donation regulations.
- **AI-Powered Insights**: Offers actionable insights and auto-generated reports for tracking ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) metrics.
- **Mobile App**: Provides a user-friendly interface for everyday food matchmaking between food providers and receivers.
- **Cost Reduction**: Helps businesses lower disposal costs and improve their brand image by contributing to social good.

## Value Propositions
- **Environmental Impact**: Contributes to reducing food waste and achieving sustainability goals.
- **Social Responsibility**: Empowers businesses and municipalities to support local communities and enhance social wellbeing.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Streamlines the food donation process, reducing costs and improving compliance.
