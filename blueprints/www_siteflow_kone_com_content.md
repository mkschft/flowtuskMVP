# KONE SiteFlow Overview

## Core Offering
KONE SiteFlow provides real-time digital tools designed for construction and building operations. It focuses on enhancing logistics management, elevator access control, and building optimization through data-driven insights.

## Key Features and Benefits
- **Real-time Control**: Offers practical tools that allow immediate management of construction time use elevators (CTUs), addressing day-to-day logistics challenges on-site.
- **Digital Access Control**: Facilitates efficient elevator access management to prioritize people movement, optimize peak hour traffic, and enhance safety by restricting access to specific floors.
- **Building Optimization**: Utilizes existing elevator data to improve building operations, including heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems, leading to reduced energy costs and consumption.
- **Energy Savings**: Claims potential savings of up to 36% on building energy consumption by leveraging accurate occupancy data.

## Target Customers and Use Cases
- **Property and Asset Managers**: Utilize SiteFlow for managing building operations and logistics efficiently.
- **Construction Companies**: Benefit from real-time tools to streamline site operations and manage elevator usage effectively.
- **Building Owners**: Use the platform for optimizing building management and reducing operational costs.

## Value Propositions
- **Enhanced Operational Efficiency**: By providing real-time insights and control, SiteFlow helps users manage logistics more effectively, leading to smoother operations.
- **Cost Reduction**: The platform aims to lower energy costs and improve resource management without the need for new equipment.
- **Safety and Security**: The digital access control feature enhances safety by managing who can access different floors within a building.
